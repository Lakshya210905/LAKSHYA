About Us

A blood bank is a place where blood collected from donation events is stored. The term "blood bank" refers to a hospital laboratory where blood products are stored and tested to reduce transfusion risks. The process requires proper management, ensuring blood bags are handled with care as they are linked to saving lives.

The development of the Web-based Blood Bank And Donation Management System (BBDMS) aims to provide a systematic way to manage blood storage and donation records. It helps both donors and those in need.

Blood Banks in India

India has a vast network of blood banks that play a crucial role in providing life-saving blood to patients in need. These blood banks are operated by government agencies, private hospitals, and non-profit organizations, ensuring a steady and safe blood supply across the country.
